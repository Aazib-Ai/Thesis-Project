Why Choose Hybrid Encryption?

High Performance

Uses AES-256 for bulk data encryption, ensuring rapid processing of large healthcare datasets without the typical homomorphic encryption bottleneck.

Secure Computation

Leverages CKKS homomorphic encryption specifically for numerical fields, allowing statistical analysis directly on encrypted data.

Privacy First

PII (Personally Identifiable Information) remains locked under AES, while analytical fields are processed blindly. The server never sees the raw data.

    Upload & Split

    Your CSV file is parsed. Sensitive identifiers (Names, IDs) are separated from numerical data (Vitals, Lab Results).

  • 2

    Hybrid Encryption

    Identifiers are sealed with AES-256. Numerical data is encrypted with CKKS, enabling mathematical operations.

  • 3

    Secure Analysis

    We perform statistical analysis (Mean, Variance, etc.) on the CKKS-encrypted data without ever decrypting it.
